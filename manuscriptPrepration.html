Guidelines for the contributer


The Contributors are requested to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • The main text should not contain the author's name or affiliation. The face sheet should contain a declaration by the author(s) stating that the article / research paper has not been published or not been submitted or accepted for publication by any other journal, magazines or publishing organization.

  • The length of the manuscript should be maximum of 5000 words including tables, figures and references along with abstract about 150-200 words. It should be typed in doubled space on A4 size pages with adequate margin 1.5 inch on all sides.

  • The cover page should contain titles of the paper, author’s name, designation, official address, e-mail id, and mobile no.

  • Tables and figures should be appropriately made and adjusted in the manuscript at a proper place. The numbering of tables, figures and graphs in the text should be clearly indicated along with a brief caption at the bottom of each figure and tile at the top of each table.

  • Authors must submit two hard copy along with a soft copy of manuscript as an e- mail attachment on journal@abes.ac.in in MS word. There should not be any difference between hard copy and electronic version of the paper.

The Specifications for formatting are-

  • Page size : 8.5” x 11.0”
  • Margins :
    • Top : 0.75”
    • Bottom : 1.0 “
    • Left : 0.75”
    • Right : 0.63”
  • Font : Times New Roman
  • Size :
    • Title : 24 pt. bold
    • Author Name : 11 pt.
    • Author details : 11 pt.
    • E-mail : 10 pt.
    • Abstract : 9 pt. bold
    • Heading : Upper Case 12 pt. bold with roman numbering
    • Sub Heading : Sentence Case 11 pt. bold with alphabet characters numbering
    • For figure labels : 8 pt. bold (Example Fig. 1 ………………….Caption)
    • For Tables : 8 pt. bold (Example TABLE 1 .…………… Caption)
    • Text : 10 pt.
    • Reference section : 9 pt.
  • Space before paragraph : 10 pt.
  • Space after paragraph : 6 pt.
  • For conclusion and reference section : no section number

  • The text box should not be used in the document. Insert all the pictures/ drawing in the documents as inline graphics. The caption of the picture/drawing should be in Times New Roman Font, 9pt Bold.
  • The articles should not contain footnotes. References should be given at the end of the manuscript and must be completed in all respect and alphabetically arranged
  • Manuscripts not consider for publication will be sent back to the author in due course of time. Acceptance of the paper for publication shall be informed after review through e-mail.
  • Contributor(s) to Journal will receive a complimentary copy of the respective issue along with 5 reprints of their article.
  • The submitted manuscript may be subjected to substantial editing so that he paper conforms to our standard.
  • Manuscripts and all editorial correspondence should be made to the Journal's Editor-in Chief.
